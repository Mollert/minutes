Gutter cleaning to be accomplished in early October. Mildew remediation pressure washing also to be done in early October. Homeowners to be notified.

Sally along with Leslie will serve as co-chairs of the Landscaping Committee.

Next board meeting set for October 20th. Annual meeting set for December 5th.
